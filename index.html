About Me

I am a petroleum engineer with a Master’s degree from Politecnico di Torino and research experience at NTNU (Norway), specializing in drilling automation, subsurface modeling, and data-driven engineering workflows.

My Master’s thesis focused on automated back-pressure control in Managed Pressure Drilling (MPD), where I developed Python-based models to stabilize bottom-hole pressure under complex operational constraints. This work strengthened my skills in numerical modeling, control-oriented thinking, and translating physical systems into reliable computational frameworks.

I am motivated to apply my background in subsurface engineering, simulation, and programming to research and engineering roles supporting low-carbon energy technologies, including CCUS, geothermal systems, and advanced subsurface applications.
